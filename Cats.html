Our Adoption Process

  • Step 1: Browse our available cats and find one that connects with you
  • Step 2: Fill out an adoption application form
  • Step 3: Meet and greet with your potential new companion
  • Step 4: Home visit to ensure a safe environment
  • Step 5: Complete the adoption and bring your new friend home!

Adoption Fee: $75 (includes vaccinations)

Why Adopt a Cat?

  • Save a life and give a deserving cat a second chance
  • Cats are independent and perfect for various lifestyles
  • Lower maintenance compared to many other pets
  • Provide companionship and reduce stress
  • All our cats are health-checked and ready for their new homes
